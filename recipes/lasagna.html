Recipe for Lasagna

Description:

"A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce."

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground beef
  • A diced onion
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two tablespoons of parsley
  • One clove of garlic
  • A dash of sugar
  • Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • Uncooked noodles
  • Cottage cheese and Parmesan for layers
  • Shredded mozzarella for topping
  • Eggs
